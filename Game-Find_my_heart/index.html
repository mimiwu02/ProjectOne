Find My Heart

Many do not know the story of the robot who had his heart stolen by Magikoopa Kamek. People only know of the story of the Super Mario Brothers. But little do they know that Mr. Robot was Magikoopa Kamek's first victim.

Objective: You and an opponent are both knights. Find Mr. Robot's heart that is hidden under one of MagiKoopa Kamek's magic carpets. Finding various gems under the carpet can increase your score. Who will be deemed the hero by finding his heart first. Finding the heart will give you access to one of Mr. Robot's memories. Collecting all of his memories will allow him to remember who he once was. A good robot! The most lovable robot in the world!
